### 1. Executive Summary Panel
Includes real-time KPI cards for **Total Employees (1,470)**, **Total Attrition (237)**, and the **Global Attrition Rate (16.1%)**.

### 2. Departmental & Role Analysis (Bar Charts)
Ranks departments by turnover. Highlights that the **Sales Department (20.6%)** and specifically **Sales Representatives (39.8%)** are the highest-risk segments.

### 3. Financial & Demographic Segmentation
Breakdown of attrition by **Income Group**, revealing a critical **25.3% churn rate** in the lowest bracket ($0-4k). Includes slicers for Gender, Marital Status, and Age Group.

### 4. Behavioral & Satisfaction Heatmaps
Visualizes the correlation between **Overtime** and churn, showing that employees working OT are **3x more likely to leave (31.5%)**. It also maps Job Satisfaction levels against attrition counts.
